Vibe Check Generator

Enter any topic, name, or URL and get a beautifully designed "vibe report" — a shareable card with a mood score, color palette, playlist suggestion, and a one-liner verdict. Think: personality test meets aesthetic mood board. Perfect for sharing on socials.

Decision Roulette

Can't decide? Type in your options and spin a beautiful animated wheel — but with a twist. It gives you a psychology-backed reason WHY it picked that one (anchoring bias, paradox of choice, etc). Educational, fun, and actually helps you commit to decisions. Shareable results.

C

Excuse Machine

Pick a situation (late to work, skipping the gym, ghosting a text) and get a gorgeously designed excuse card — ranging from "barely believable" to "Oscar-worthy". Beautiful typography, fun animations, and a share button so you can send it to the person you're excusing yourself to.

D

Life Stats Dashboard

Enter your birthday and get a stunning, animated dashboard showing wild stats about your life: heartbeats so far, sunsets witnessed, hours spent sleeping, how many times you've orbited the sun. Beautiful data viz with a "share your stats" card generator.
