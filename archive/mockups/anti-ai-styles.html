The Receipt. Thermal printer aesthetic. Monospace type, dashed lines, fake barcode. Feels like getting a bill for your career anxiety. The format is instantly recognisable and inherently shareable — receipt-style posts go viral constantly. "THANK YOU FOR YOUR ANXIETY" is the kind of copy that makes people screenshot.
